Objective:

The objective of the game is to choose a move (Rock, Paper, or Scissors) that defeats the computer's move. Each round, you and the computer will simultaneously select one of the three options, and the winner will be determined based on the following rules:


How to Play:

  • Choose Your Move:
    • At the start of each round, you will be prompted to select one of the three available moves: Rock Paper Scissors
  • Computer's Move:
    • Once you make your choice, the computer will randomly select one of the three moves.
  • Comparison:
    • The game will automatically compare your choice with the computer's choice and display the outcome.

Winning Conditions:

  • Rock beats Scissors (Rock crushes Scissors).
  • Scissors beats Paper (Scissors cut Paper).
  • Paper beats Rock (Paper covers Rock).
  • If you and the computer choose the same move, the round is a tie.

Regulations:

  • Fair Play:
    • The game uses a random algorithm to generate the computer's move, ensuring that each round is fair and based on chance.
  • Moves: You can only choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors each round. Once selected, your move cannot be changed during that round.
  • No Cheating: The game is designed to be purely based on random chance. There are no advantages or biases for either the player or the computer.
  • Respect and Fun: This game is intended to be a fun and casual experience. Play with good sportsmanship and enjoy the simplicity of Rock-Paper-Scissors!

Enjoy the Game!

Remember, Rock-Paper-Scissors is a quick and fun game, and no matter the outcome, it's all about having fun. Enjoy playing, and good luck!
